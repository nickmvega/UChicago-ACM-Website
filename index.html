Welcome to UChicago ACM

Mission Statement

UChicago ACM strives to foster communication and collaboration within the University's computer science community and other technical communities, supporting members' professional growth through opportunities for lifelong learning, career development, and professional networking. We plan on organizing various events, including round-table discussions, guest lectures, gaming nights, and our unique ‘innovate evenings’.

Why you should join

Join UChicago ACM and become a part of a vibrant community dedicated to fostering communication and 
collaboration within the University's computer science community and other technical fields. 
Our inclusive environment welcomes everyone, regardless of coding experience.
                                                
As a member, you'll benefit from:
                                                
- Opportunities for Lifelong Learning: Gain valuable insights and expand your knowledge through engaging 
  activities.
- Career Development: Avail yourself of resources to support your professional growth and career 
  aspirations.
- Professional Networking: Connect with like-minded individuals and industry professionals.
- Diverse Events: Participate in various events, from round-table discussions to guest lectures, gaming 
  nights, and our unique ‘innovate evenings.’
                                                
Join us in building a community where passion for technology meets inclusivity and learning. No coding 
experience required – just a desire to explore, connect, and have fun!

work on publishing papers at ML conferences and workshops. Additionally, the ML Committee will work on
