Mission Statement

ACM Uchicago strives to foster communication and collaboration within the University's computer science community and other technical communities, supporting members' professional growth through opportunities for lifelong learning, career development, and professional networking. We plan on organizing various events, including round-table discussions, guest lectures, gaming nights, and our unique ‘innovate evenings’.
